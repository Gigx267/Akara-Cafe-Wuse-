About Us

A mix of Nigerian, English and American breakfast basics - with Akara as staple, to city dwellers seeking easy, affordable daily meal options and guests seeking a nostalgic/reminiscent breakfast experience. ​

We take pride in using only the freshest ingredients in our dishes. Our food is made from scratch daily to ensure the highest quality and taste.

Our Features

In addition to our in-house dining options, we also offer catering services for events and parties. We can provide delicious and authentic Nigerian breakfast dishes that are sure to impress your guests.

Our Facilities

Our cafe is designed to be warm and welcoming, with a cozy atmosphere that invites you to relax and enjoy your meal
